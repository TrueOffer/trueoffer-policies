TrueOffer Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 10, 2025

1. What We Collect

  • Names, contact details, and property details from form submissions

2. How We Use It

  • To match property sellers with interested investors

We do not sell your data. We only share submitted data with verified platform users for the purpose of matching.

Data is stored securely using third-party platforms with encryption and access controls (e.g., Airtable, Tally.so).

5. Your Rights

  • You may request to view or delete your data at any time by contacting us
  • You may opt out of communications at any time

6. Cookies & Analytics

Our platform may use basic tracking (cookies or analytics) to improve your experience.

7. Updates

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of major changes via email or in-app notifications.
